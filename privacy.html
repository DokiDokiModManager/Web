Doki Doki Mod Manager - Privacy Policy

Last updated 16th February 2019

This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by the Doki Doki Mod Manager website (accessible at https://doki.space), application ("app") and related services, and how I as the developer use this information.

If you have additional questions or require more information about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact me through email at zudo@doki.space.

Website

Personal information

The Doki Doki Mod Manager website does not collect any information that may be used to identify a particular person.

Non-personal information

Doki Doki Mod Manager follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include, but are not limited to, internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

Cookies and other data

The website may store cookies on your computer in order to provide or enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files that persist even after you close your browser. Contrary to common opinion, cookies are not inherently malicious and often simply serve as a way to remember user preferences.

In addition to cookies set by Doki Doki Mod Manager, other cookies may be set by third parties, in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. To know more detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers, it can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

These third parties may process certain information including, but not limited to,internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Application

Personal information

To provide certain services, including syncing preferences and save files, Doki Doki Mod Manager collects certain personally identifying information. This includes email addresses, usernames, internet protocol (IP) addresses and copies of your DDLC save files. If you wish to have this information removed, please contact me at the email address listed at the top of this document.

Error reporting and feedback

In order to efficiently resolve any issues ("bugs") in the Doki Doki Mod Manager app, some information may be collected in the event of a crash or error, or when you decide to send feedback. This information includes, but is not limited to, internet protocol (IP) addresses, operating system (OS) type, information about the hardware in your computer and relevant filenames, which may also include personal information, depending on how your computer is set up. Rest assured that this information is only used for the purpose of resolving technical issues, and not for any malicious or commercial purposes.
