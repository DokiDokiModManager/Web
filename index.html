About

Doki Doki Mod Manager! was born after I saw the way mods were being distributed in the DDLC community. Just .zip files, with no properly defined structure. DDMM aims to fix this by:

  • Creating a universal structure for DDLC mods.
  • Keeping mod installs separate, so you don't have to delete save files from your old games.

Mod Store

Doki Doki Mod Manager! has its own mod store, built directly into the app. Upload your mods for free and anyone can find and install it with the click of a button.
